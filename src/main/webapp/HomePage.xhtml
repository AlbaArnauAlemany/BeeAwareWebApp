Pollenic Allergy Monitoring Platform
Home Page

Allergies affect a large portion of the population, and their symptoms can be exacerbated by various environmental factors, including weather conditions.

The platform's goal is to provide users with a channel for information on high-risk periods based on daily pollen forecasts to help them anticipate potential symptoms.

The platform will offer users the ability to log their perceived symptoms daily on a subjective scale. By tracking this data, users can compare their symptoms against the pollen load index to identify potential patterns. Additionally, users can record days when they take antihistamines to observe how medication impacts their symptoms. This feature will allow for personalized insights and informed decision-making.

All collected information will be grouped into a graph, offering a simple and effective visualization tool. This graph will display personal history, including symptom severity, pollen index, and antihistamine use. Users can review these insights to adjust their behavior accordingly or share the data with a healthcare professional. The platform will leverage the Google Pollen API to retrieve the daily pollen load based on the user's specified location, ensuring accurate and relevant information.
